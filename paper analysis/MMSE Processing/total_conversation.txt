  Key takeaways:

  Section V (Non-Linear Decoding):
  - MMSE-SIC achieves the MAC sum capacity: SSE = (1-τₚ/τc) 𝔼{log₂ det(Iₖ +
  PĤᴴE⁻¹Ĥ)}
  - But the gain over linear MMSE is only ~1% — negligible!
  - Reason: Favorable propagation makes user channels nearly orthogonal in
  cell-free mMIMO, so MMSE already cancels virtually all interference
  - Non-linear processing is NOT needed — a great practical result

  Section VI (Fronthaul Signaling):
  - Counterintuitive result: Level 4 (centralized) needs LESS fronthaul than
  Levels 2/3 with star topology, because Levels 2/3 expand N-dimensional signals
